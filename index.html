What is Heylo?

Our app is designed to provide a place where users can be heard without talking to someone. Users have the option to text or talk to the app and through the use of natural language processing and sentiment analysis, provided by the google API, the app will output a tailored response.

Our demo of the app was made using Voiceflow. Unfornately, we were unable to intergrate the Google Natural Lanaguage Processing API, so we simulated its capabalities by creating simple mock databases of key words and analyzed the users response with them. Once we analyzed the responses, the app replies with a relevant response. To simulate the AI involved, the response is generated from some mock databases of responses we created. These databases are categorized by emotion, so once the user's response is analyzed the appropriate response database is access.
